  	 		3 &0\\
  	 		0&1
  	 		\end{bmatrix}$ and define $T: \RR^2 \to \RR^2$ by $T(\bx) = A\bx$.  Let $\bu = \begin{bmatrix} 1\\-1 \end{bmatrix}$ and compute $T(\bu)$.\\
  	 		
 $$T(\bu) = \left[ \begin{array}{c} \answer{3} \\ \answer{-1} \end{array} \right]$$

 
  	
  		\end{question}
